Independent Project


For my independent project, I would like to create a magic eight ball. It will be a website that will be of mostly vector images. When the user clicks on the magic eight ball, the ball will shake and the appropriate responses of a magic eight ball will appear. I hope to implement something that will allow the user to customize the color scheme of the magic eight ball as well. I am also thinking of adding a background (comprised colorful vector images) to make the website look like an office of somesort.

However, if that seems too simple, which I'm afraid it might be, perhaps I could make a how-to website. Since I'm currently watching the Australian Open, I could make a website explaining how-to play tennis.

Main User and Goals
User: Someone who is unable to make a decision or wants to leave their decisions up to fate.
Goals: To be able to help someone make a decision using a magic eight ball without physically having a magic eight ball.
